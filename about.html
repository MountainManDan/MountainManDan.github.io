About Mountain Man Dan!

Greetings!

My name is Daniel Wilson and I am a graduate student in physics at the University of Maine and an avid adventurer. When I am not in the lab, I decompress by biking, cross country skiing, rowing, fly fishing, acrylic painting, hiking, or fossil hunting. I became a member of the AMC Four Thousand Footer Club by completing all the New Hampshire 48 four thousand footers in less than two years and have been slowly pecking away at my winter 48. I thoroughly enjoy capturing the most beautiful moments while tramping through the woods and it is my wish to share these moments of solace with the world through my photography, painting, or woodworking.
